# MVP Scope

## MVP

Authentication; Dashboard; Projects; Tasks; Subtasks; Checklist; custom task statuses; Kanban, List, Calendar; My Tasks; Tags; time tracking; attachments; comments; activity history; search; templates; reports cơ bản; archive; settings.

## Post-MVP

Small-team collaboration, notification nâng cao, tích hợp công cụ và reporting nâng cao.

## Out of Scope

Billing, subscription, AI assistant, public marketplace, video call, realtime multiplayer editing, enterprise permission system, team chat và native mobile app.

## Future Consideration

Chọn object storage (Cloudinary, R2, S3 hoặc Supabase Storage) dựa trên chi phí và yêu cầu upload.
